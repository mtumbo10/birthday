Magician Card Game List

Round 1: Fun Connection (Warming up - honestly more boring but breaks the ice)

2♠: Do you prefer coffee or tea? With what add-ins (e.g., sugar, milk)?
3♠: How many speeding tickets do you think I’ve gotten before?
4♠: Have you ever been fired from a job before?
5♠: How many times have you been in love?
6♠: Were you popular in school (elementary, middle school, high school, college…reader picks one)
7♠: Did you enjoy what you studied in school? Why or why not?
8♠: Which 3 places would you love to travel to and why?
9♠: What is your last listened to song?
10♠: What show have you seen most recently? Does this fit your usual watching habits?
J♠: What book have you read most recently? Does this fit your usual book genre preferences?
Q♠: What is the best piece of advice you have ever been given?
K♠: Describe your perfect date.
A♠: What job did you want to have growing up?

2♦: Who is your favorite music artist?
3♦: What compliment do you hear the most?
4♦: What is your favorite childhood memory?
5♦: What is your favorite memory?
6♦: If you were to take a new job, what would it be?
7♦: Are there any cities you are itching to live in?
8♦: What life experiences are you hoping to have?
9♦: What would your ideal adventure be? Why?
10♦: What is your most toxic trait?
J♦: What do you think your best trait is?
Q♦: What is the best gift you have ever received? Why did it mean so much to you?
K♦: What are you most afraid of?
A♦: How would you define your values?

2♣: What brings you joy?
3♣: What are your biggest pet peeves?
4♣: What were your favorite shows/cartoons growing up?
5♣: Finish this sentence: When I was a kid (like elementary school), you could find me _____.
6♣: Do you have siblings? How many? Do you get along?
7♣: What is the most embarrassing memory you have?
8♣: What is a non-negotiable in your life?
9♣: What is your favorite song lyric you can think of off the top of your head? Why do you like it so much?
10♣: What is your favorite hobby?
J♣: What is your favorite way to express creativity?
Q♣: What are you currently not giving enough time to?
K♣: If you could write a book, what would you write it about (i.e., what belief do you have that you would want to share)
A♣: What are you most jealous of?

2♥: Are you normally early, on time, or late to things?
3♥: When do you feel most alive?
4♥: What is your favorite and least favorite foods? Are there any fan-favorites you hate or vis-a-vis?
5♥: What do you love so much you forget about time?
6♥: How important are birthdays and holidays to you? Which is your favorite?
7♥: Would you consider yourself a fashion person? What do your clothes say about you?
8♥: Are you more creative or analytical?
9♥: What is your best hidden talent?
10♥: How would you define yourself in 3 words?
J♥: Do you have tattoos? If not, would you get one?
Q♥: What is the most impulsive thing you have ever done?
K♥: What is the best thing you’ve ever thought about someone else?
A♥: What is the worst thing you’ve ever thought about someone else?

Round 2: A ~bit~ deeper Connection

2♠: What has been most on your mind recently?
3♠: When was the last time you felt lucky to be you?
4♠: What is the happiest moment you have had over the last year?
5♠: Do you think you have good boundaries? What do you think is your weakest boundary and why?
6♠: What is the last thing you lied about?
7♠: What are your current priorities for this stage of your life? Why? What title would you give this chapter?
8♠: In one word, how would you describe your relationship with yourself?
9♠: What do you wish people noticed about you more?
10♠: Are you lying to yourself about anything?
J♠: What lesson took you the longest to un-learn?
Q♠: Do you think your perception of yourself matches other people's perceptions?
K♠: What is the worst pain you ever felt that wasn't physical?
A♠: Who is someone from your past that you miss? Why did you stop talking with them?

2♦: What do you need more of in your life?
3♦: What do you need less of in your life?
4♦: How do you know you can trust someone?
5♦: Would you rather been seen as kind, smart, or attractive?
6♦: What keeps you up at night?
7♦: Has a stranger ever changed your life?
8♦: What is the best quality/most beautiful thing about your mom? What is her name?
9♦: What is your favorite quality about your dad? What is his name?
10♦: What belief do you disagree with most with your parents/family/siblings?
J♦: Which traits from your parents do you want to keep and which do you want to let go of?
Q♦: What do you think your parents taught you about love?
K♦: How do you feel about where you grew up?
A♦: What do you think surprises people most about how you grew up?

2♣: What is one moment that shaped who you are today?
3♣: Name and describe a time where you were strongest you have ever been?
4♣: Do you think belief in fate/think life is pre-determined?
5♣: If you could leave a mark on the world, what would it be and why?
6♣: What was the most meaningful purchase you have ever made?
7♣: What part of yourself are you most hesitant to share with other people?
8♣: What are you still exploring within yourself?
9♣: What polarizing belief do you have? Why are you so passionate about it?
10♣: What about your current self would most surprise your younger self?
J♣: What was something that was said to you as a kid that you still carry with you? If you could go back, what would you say to the younger version of you?
Q♣: Do you think you have ever let go of a dream? If so, what was it? How do you feel about it now?
K♣: What have you realized is more important to you than previous thought before?
A♣: What is the biggest mistake you have ever made? What did you learn from it?

2♥: What is your biggest insecurity?
3♥: Do you think you have any insecurities or fears that accidentally hurt others? If so, what are they?
4♥: Do you think people are capable of (really) “changing” the world? If so, should they?
5♥: What do you think is the best way to make another person feel? Can you recall the last time you felt like that?
6♥: What do you think is the worst way to make another person feel? Can you recall the last time you felt that way?
7♥: If you could change one thing about yourself, what would it be and why?
8♥: How much responsibility do you think you have in your life? What are the main pieces of that responsibility?
9♥: How much control do you think you have over your life path, from 0 - 100%? Why?
10♥: What is your biggest source of pride?
J♥: In one word, describe how being in love feels?
Q♥: What feelings or thoughts are the most difficult for you to communicate? How do you prefer to communicate in these times?
K♥: What fear holds you back the most?
A♥: What is the most important lesson you have learned over the past year?

Round 3: How Others See You (to be answered by the player [you] about the person [me] reading the card)

Note: Make sure to ignore all J-A cards, they have no value in this round.

2♠: What is something you really admire about me?
3♠: What is something you recommend I let go of?
4♠: Complete the sentence: I really appreciate that you ____
5♠: Is there anything you still don't know about me that you wish you did?
6♠: What answer of mine surprised you the most throughout the game?
7♠: What was your initial perception of me and how has it shifted?
8♠: What don't you think I give myself enough credit for?
9♠: How do you think we complement each other?
10♠: What about me is the hardest thing for you to understand?

2♦: Based on my answers, would you recommend any books to me?
3♦: Based on my answers, would you recommend any TV shows/movies to me?
4♦: Based on my answers, would you recommend any podcasts to me?
5♦: What do you think I am best at?
6♦: What is one thing you wish I knew?
7♦: What do you think I'm going to do in the future?
8♦: What do you love doing with me?
9♦: What do you dread doing with me?
10♦: Admit something.

2♣: In one word, describe how you feel right now?
3♣: Have I helped you change your perspective on anything? If so, what?
4♣: Do I remind you of anyone else you know?
5♣: Do I intimidate you? Why or why not?
6♣: What job do you think I am best suited for?
7♣: Complete the sentence: My favorite qualities about you are —,—, and —
8♣: What reminds you of me?
9♣: What is something you think you will remember about me from this game?
10♣: What do you think my biggest weakness is?

2♥: What do you think my biggest strength is?
3♥: What do you think I am most unaware of about myself?
4♥: What was your favorite answer of mine? How did it make you feel?
5♥: Which of my answers did you disagree with the most? Why?
6♥: What are you most grateful for in this current moment?
7♥: What questions were you most scared to answer?
8♥: How would you describe me to someone we didn’t know?
9♥: What would be the perfect gift for me?
10♥: If you made a playlist for me, what 1-3 songs would be on it?
