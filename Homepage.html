Homepage

Welcome!

Hoi! Welcome to the insanely archaic, coded attempt at a scavenger hunt. The scavenger hunt is broken into 3 puzzles listed at the top of the page. Each puzzle will either unlock geographical locations or games. Scrolling your mouse over each puzzle creates a drop-down list with 3 options:

  1. Puzzle: The actual puzzle and instructions
  2. Solution Checker: A checker to confirm your answers
  3. Solution: For AFTER you finish the puzzle - includes the solution and any important information you'll need for next steps
NOTE: The solution page is basically just the answers, so if the coding is wrong, you can just go straight here, but fingers crossed cause it's not as fun

Puzzles #1 and #2 will send you on an adventure to pick up materials. Puzzle #3 will unlock the rules of a new card game...magician (hahaha).

Time Estimate

I'm estimating puzzle #1/2 will likely take you ~1hr walking or 30min biking to go, pick-up everything, and get back. The furthest place is 25 minutes away (walking, only 7 biking), but I'm assuming you might run into some yappers so I built in extra time to the estimate. Honestly, I am realizing I didn't think of possible hand restrictions, so I would either plan to bike with a backpack/basket or take a friend or two. It should also be easy to split trips, but alack alas lmk if there are issues.
